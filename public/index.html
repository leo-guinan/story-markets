The problem

Burns destroy value.
They don't have to.

Every deflationary burn is an act of conviction — a project so certain of its own worth it destroys supply to protect it. But the burn address forgets everything.

Before

Tokens → void → silence

The burn executes. Supply contracts. The narrative dissolves. Nobody writes it down. The project that burned 10M tokens for its community gets a transaction hash and nothing else.

0x000000000000000000000000000000000000dead
↑ this address remembers nothing
After

Tokens → story → market → legacy

The burn becomes a stake. A story is written — what happened, who did it, what it meant. The market decides if it matters. The ledger records the outcome permanently, regardless of result.

How it works

Four steps.
One permanent record.

The mechanism is simple. The record is immutable. The story compounds whether you win or lose.
